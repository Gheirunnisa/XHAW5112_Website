About Us

Our SME was founded with a mission to empower individuals who may not have had access to formal education by equipping them with practical, marketable skills. We believe that skill development is the key to unlocking opportunities, increasing employability, and fostering entrepreneurship within our communities.

Through our tailored training programs, we enhance the capabilities of domestic workers and gardeners, helping them become more valuable in the job market or thrive as independent service providers. By investing in people, we aim to create a ripple effect of empowerment, confidence, and economic growth in the communities we serve.

What the Organization Plans to Uphold

Empowerment Through Education: The organization is committed to supporting individuals, especially those without access to formal education, by providing practical, marketable skills that enhance their independence and self-reliance.

Community Development: By training domestic workers, gardeners, and other community members, the organization strengthens the local workforce, increases employability, and encourages entrepreneurship.

Quality Training: All courses are designed to provide hands-on experience combined with essential theoretical knowledge, ensuring learners acquire skills that are both practical and professional.
