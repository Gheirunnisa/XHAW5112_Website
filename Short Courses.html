Short-Term Programs

Our short-term courses are designed for individuals looking to quickly gain practical skills that can be applied immediately in the workplace or at home. Each course lasts 6 weeks and costs R750, focusing on hands-on learning to ensure students gain both knowledge and confidence in a condensed period. These courses offer flexible schedules and small class sizes, making them ideal for working adults or learners with limited time. Examples include domestic skills and housekeeping, basic gardening and landscaping, life skills essentials, and first aid fundamentals. They are perfect for anyone wanting fast-track learning to enhance employability or improve personal skill sets.
