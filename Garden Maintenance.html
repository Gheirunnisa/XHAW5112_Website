The Garden Maintenance Course is designed to provide learners with practical skills to maintain and improve gardens and outdoor spaces. Participants will learn about plant care, siol management, pruning techniques, lawn and garden upkeep, and pest control methods. The course also covers the safe and effective use of gardening tools and equipment, as well as basic landscaping principles to create attractive, healthy outdoor environments. By the end of the program, learners will be equipped with the knowledge and hands-on experience to care for gardens professionally or enhance their own outdoor spaces, fostering both creativity and sustainability.

What You’ll Learn

  • Plant Care & Growth
  • Soil Preparation & Maintenance
  • Pruning & Landscaping
  • Lawn & Garden Upkeep
  • Pest & Disease Management

Why Choose This Course?

This course offers hands-on training in gardening and landscaping, teaching practical skills that can lead to employment or enhance your personal outdoor spaces. Learners will gain knowledge in plant care, soil management, pruning, and sustainable gardening practices, while also boosting creativity and confidence to maintain healthy and attractive gardens.
